Content Structure & Scannability

Modern users scan websites instead of reading them word-for-word. The old site utilizes dense paragraphs of text with no spacing or heading hierarchy, resulting in high exit rates.

Is information easy to scan?

Old Site: No. Text wraps across full width of screen without cards or highlights. Critical statements are buried under legacy tables.

New Site: Card Layout

New Redesign: Yes. Content is modularized using distinct blocks with clear margins, custom dividers, and high-readability fonts.

7

Visual Hierarchy & CTA Placement

Important information must attract the eye first. The new prototype establishes a strict visual flow: First the CBSE affiliation status, then a principal quote banner, followed by fee details, and ending with quick admissions calls.

Visual Focus: The old website header has a flat contrast color layout (Everything has equal weight). The new redesign highlights the "Student Portal" and "Enroll online" buttons using bright, highly distinct button colors, directing parent focus.
